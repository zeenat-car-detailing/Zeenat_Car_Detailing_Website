About Zeenat

Redefining car care in Lahore with premium mobile detailing services that bring the showroom shine to your doorstep.

Our Mission

Quality Meets Convenience

Zeenat Car Detailing was founded with a simple yet ambitious goal: to provide the highest quality car care services in Lahore without the inconvenience of traditional workshops. We understand that your time is valuable, which is why we've revolutionized the industry by bringing professional-grade detailing directly to your home or office.

Using only premium imported products from Germany and Japan, we ensure that every vehicle we treat receives the best possible care. From basic washes to advanced ceramic coatings and Paint Protection Film (PPF), our certified technicians treat every car as if it were their own.
